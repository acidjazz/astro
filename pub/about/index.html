ASTRO's 5D PROCESS
Astro's proven 5-stage design process is powered by our special blend of insights, tools and talent. Through design, we attempt to unify the dimensional relationships of time and space around the brands, products, and experiences we create.
DIALOG

We meet and discuss the goals, parameters and opportunities of the project to help craft the program and identify the best internal and network resources required for a successful outcome.

Activities may include meetings, brainstorming, conference calls, networking, and proposals.

DEFINE

As we begin the design work, Astro will create a strong point-of-view by immersing our team in relevant data from clients, competitors, market insights, and usage models to fuel the design process.

Activities may include research, demographics, audits, technology reviews, user profiles & user scenarios, engineering & manufacturing reviews.

DESIGN

Using the fuel from the Define phase, Astro will create a range of concepts that explore the design and innovation potential by using various communication and design tools over a number of collaborative review sessions.

Activities may include 2D & 3D storyboards, identity concepts, illustrations, CAD models, physical models & prototypes.

DEVELOP

Often overlapping with the Design phase, Astro will collaborate with implementation resources like engineering, manufacturing, printing, and coding, to guide and subsequently transfer the chosen direction into production.

Activities may include 2D/3D CAD development, color, materials & finishes specification files, packaging, out-of-box experiences, UI/UX refinement and interaction guidance.

DEPLOY

Astro will help advocate and realize the original vision of the program into full commercialization mode with chosen resources and partners.

Activities might include part, product or print reviews, manufacturer visits, sales, advertising or retail support.
